Dr. Shedrack Akpe joined the Yoon Group in March 2025 as a postdoc at Baylor University after obtaining his PhD in Chemical Engineering in 2023. During his time in the Yoon Group, his research interests focused on studying catalytic processes on biomass conversion using DFT calculations and microkinetic modeling. He will continue his postdoc research at Baylor University.
