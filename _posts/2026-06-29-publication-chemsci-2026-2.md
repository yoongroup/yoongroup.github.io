We’re excited to share that our collaborative paper is now published in [Chemical Science](https://doi.org/10.1039/D6SC03158G)!

This work is a wonderful theory–experiment collaboration between the Yoon Group and the Martin Group at Baylor University, initiated and led by Dr. Caleb Martin. Bohak served as a co-corresponding author on the study.

Congratulations to the co-lead authors, Kanika and Ragene from the Martin Group, and many thanks to Dr. Martin for the opportunity to collaborate on this exciting work!
